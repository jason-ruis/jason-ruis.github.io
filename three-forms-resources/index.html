About Three Forms Resources

Three Forms Resources is a curated directory of trusted Reformed materials related to the Three Forms of Unity — the Heidelberg Catechism, the Belgic Confession, and the Canons of Dort.

Our mission is to serve the Church by making it easier to discover books, study guides, commentaries, sermons, and other resources that help believers grow in their understanding of these foundational Reformed confessions.

Each resource has been carefully cataloged with detailed information including author, publisher, description, and links to access or purchase the material.
