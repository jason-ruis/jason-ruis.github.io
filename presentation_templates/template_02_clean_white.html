The Foundation of Knowledge

Every person must answer the fundamental question: What serves as your ultimate authority for determining truth?

This choice shapes our entire understanding of reality, morality, and meaning.

Core Questions to Consider

  • How do we determine what is true?
  • What is the source of moral standards?
  • How do we gain reliable knowledge?
  • What gives life ultimate meaning?

These questions reveal our fundamental worldview commitments.

Two Approaches to Knowledge

Autonomous Reasoning

  • Human reason as final authority
  • Empirical evidence as primary source
  • Self-determination of meaning
  • Relative moral standards

Biblical Authority

  • Scripture as ultimate standard
  • Divine revelation as foundation
  • God-given purpose and meaning
  • Absolute moral principles
