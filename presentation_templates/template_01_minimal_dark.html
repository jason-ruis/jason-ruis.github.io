What is Ultimate Authority?

Every person operates from some foundational belief about what serves as their ultimate authority for truth and knowledge.

This foundational assumption shapes how we interpret everything we encounter in life.

Three Essential Questions

  • What is the source of ultimate authority?
  • How do we gain true knowledge?
  • What provides meaning and purpose?

These questions reveal our fundamental worldview assumptions.

Worldview Comparison

Secular Worldview

  • Human reason as ultimate authority
  • Material universe is all that exists
  • Knowledge through empirical observation
  • Meaning is self-constructed

Biblical Worldview

  • Scripture as ultimate authority
  • God created and sustains all reality
  • Knowledge begins with fear of the Lord
  • Meaning found in God's purposes
