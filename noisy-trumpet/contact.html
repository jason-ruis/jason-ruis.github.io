Frequently Asked Questions

How can I order books in bulk for my church or organization? +

We offer special pricing for bulk orders of 10 or more copies. Please email orders@noisytrumpet.com with the titles and quantities you're interested in, and we'll provide a custom quote with discounted pricing and shipping options.

Do you accept speaking engagements or podcast interviews? +

Yes! We're open to speaking engagements at churches, conferences, and Reformed gatherings, as well as podcast and media interviews. Please email speaking@noisytrumpet.com with details about your event or program, including date, location, and topic of interest.

When will my pre-ordered items ship? +

Pre-ordered items will ship according to their listed release dates. You'll receive an email notification when your order ships. Digital versions (if included in your order) will be delivered immediately upon release.

What is your refund policy? +

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. Physical products may be returned within 30 days of receipt for a full refund if they're in original condition. Digital products are non-refundable after download. Please contact us if you have any concerns about your order.

Do you offer international shipping? +

Currently, we ship physical products within the United States and Canada. Digital products are available worldwide. If you're located internationally and interested in our physical products, please contact us to discuss options.

How can I submit a manuscript for consideration? +

At this time, we're primarily focused on our current publishing schedule. However, we're always interested in hearing about quality Reformed theological works. Please email info@noisytrumpet.com with a brief description of your manuscript and we'll let you know if we're accepting submissions.
