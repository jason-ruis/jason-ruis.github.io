About This Work

The Christian Reformed Church in North America stands at a crossroads. Recent decades have witnessed significant theological drift from our historic confessional moorings, raising urgent questions about the future of Reformed witness in our denomination.

This pamphlet examines the current challenges facing confessional integrity within the CRCNA, offering both diagnosis and hope. Drawing from Scripture, our historic confessions, and the wisdom of Reformed theologians past and present, it charts a course toward genuine renewal—not compromise disguised as progress, but authentic return to biblical faithfulness.

Key Topics Addressed:

  • The Authority of Scripture in denominational decision-making
  • Understanding our confessional standards in contemporary context
  • The role of church assemblies in maintaining doctrinal integrity
  • Biblical approaches to cultural engagement vs. accommodation
  • Practical steps toward confessional renewal
  • Learning from historical precedents of church reform
